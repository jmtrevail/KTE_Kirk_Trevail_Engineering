Meet the Team

Steven Kirk CEng

Steven is a seasoned engineer with a proven track record in delivering innovative design and analysis solutions across complex engineering challenges. Having played critical roles in offshore wind, energy storage and construction projects, he helps clients navigate technical complexity with confidence and precision. His history of technology development include leading the mechanical design of the world’s first gravitational energy storage system and the mechanical and structural design of Scotland’s first satellite, UKube-1. Whether supporting early-stage product development or providing in-depth structural analysis, Steven offers a rare combination of technical depth, practical insight, and team leadership that drives projects forward.

Jack Trevail CEng

Jack brings creativity and innovation to engineering, with a passion for developing meaningful ideas and concepts into robust proven technology. He thrives on solving complex multidisciplinary engineering challenges, and has sustained high performance delivery technical projects across a range of sectors throughout the engineering development process, from concept generation to in-production optimisation. From consumer products at Dyson, award winning flight simulator design for Rolls-Royce, to hydrogen storage at Gravitricity, he brings a range of expertise, driven to enact change through engineering.
